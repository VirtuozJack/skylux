ABOUT US

Skyluxe Roofing & Sheet Metal is a trusted company that serves the Greater Toronto Area. Our efforts to exceed our clients’ expectations helped us earn a positive reputation in the community.

We provide comprehensive ICI (Industrial, Commercial, and Institutional) and Residential roofing and sheet metal services. These services range from roofing inspections to repairs to 24-hour emergency services.

Unlike other roofing companies, we strive to meet our clients’ needs in a personalized way. We value your satisfaction, and that is why we work closely with you.
